ABOUT THE PROJECT

A short documentary and a tribute to two climbers pushing their limits under the Perseid Meteor Shower. Climbing in complete darkness with only a headlamp to guide them, they faced the unknown—navigating the rock, battling insects, and embracing the vastness of the night sky.
