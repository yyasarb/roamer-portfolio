ABOUT THE PROJECT

A founder’s story capturing the journey behind Liberté, a brand inspired by the raw and untamed flavors of the Arctic. This film brings the audience closer to the vision, passion, and craftsmanship that define its unique taste.

‍My Role: Pre-production, Filming, Color Grading, and Editing. I shaped the visual narrative from concept to final cut, blending atmospheric cinematography with refined color work to reflect the Arctic’s essence and the brand’s identity.
