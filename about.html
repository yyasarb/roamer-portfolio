HELLO WORLD

Climbing trees. Hiking mountains. Falling, then climbing again. Chasing the perfect shot with my 35mm. The sea always calling me back. My cat Bella by my side. The wind in the grass. Reaching for every branch. Searching for new perspectives. A camera in hand from an early age. Passion for film and cinematography. Capturing moments, telling stories. Each frame part of the adventure.
