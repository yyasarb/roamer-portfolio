I always pack backup batteries, propellers, memory cards, and a secondary drone. For critical shoots, I’ll bring a spare controller and tablet too. It is crucial to have redundancy when filming internationally.

When rain throws a curveball, I’ll pivot to ground-level filming or reschedule. I always monitor forecasts and plan backup dates. If needed, I’ll maximize brief weather windows between storms.

Yes, I can film in sub-zero temperatures, but I’ll need to take extra precautions with batteries, equipment protection, and safety protocols. I’ve got specialized cold-weather gear and experience handling winter shooting conditions.
